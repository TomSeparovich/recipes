Creamy Chicken Fajita Pasta

Ingredients (Makes 4 Serves)

  • 600g Chicken Breast
  • 30g Fajita Seasoning
  • 1 Red Onion
  • 200g Capsicums
  • 100g Light Cream Cheese
  • 320g Pasta

Steps

  1. Slice Onion and Capsicums up, leave to the side
  2. To a pan on high heat add Diced Chicken Breast with 15g of Fajita Seasoning and mix
  3. Let the Chicken cook
  4. Add Sliced Red Onion, Capsicum and remaining Fajita Seasoning to the pan and mix
  5. Cook Pasta
  6. Add Light Cream Cheese to the pan with some Pasta water on low heat
  7. Add Cooked Pasta with another cup of pasta water
