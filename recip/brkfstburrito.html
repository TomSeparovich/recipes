Ingredients (Makes 4 Serves)

  • 100g Diced Bacon
  • 4 Extra Lean Beef Sausages
  • 1 Onion
  • 8 Eggs
  • 4 Tortilla
  • 120g Cheese
  • 160g Mushroom
  • Milk
  • Hot Sauce (Optional)

Steps

  1. Dice onion and chop mushroom and sausage up
  2. Put bacon onto pan on high heat
  3. Add sausage and lower to medium heat
  4. Add onion and mushroom
  5. Once cooked, pour evenly into the tortillas
  6. Add cheese to the tortillas
  7. Add eggs with milk into a bowl and whisk
  8. Cook scrambled eggs in a pan on low heat
  9. Once cooked, pour evenly into the tortillas
  10. Optionally add hot sauce to the tortillas
  11. Fold tortillas up and fry on top and bottom on high heat
