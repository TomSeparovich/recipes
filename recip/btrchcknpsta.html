Ingredients (Makes 4 Serves)

  • 550g Chicken Breast
  • 2 Tbsp Garam Masala
  • 2 Tbsp Smoked Paprika
  • 2 Tbsp Tumeric
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 5g Ginger
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 145g Low Fat Greek Yoghurt
  • 15g Light Butter
  • Half an Onion
  • 350g Chopped Tomato
  • 280g Elbow Pasta
  • 55g Light Cheese

Steps

  1. Mix Diced Chicken Breast with 1 Tbsp of each spice (Garam Masala, Smoked Paprika, Tumeric), Salt and Pepper, Ginger, Garlic and 90g Yoghurt
  2. Slice onion
  3. Put Butter and Onion in a pan on medium heat
  4. Add Tomato and mix
  5. Add remaing spices and yoghurt and mix
  6. Let cool for 5 minutes
  7. Blend until smooth
  8. Cook Pasta
  9. Cook Chicken mix on medium heat
  10. Add Sauce and Cheese on low heat and mix
  11. Add pasta and mix
